## 🧠 SUMMARY (KIẾN THỨC + DOMAIN PMI ĐƯỢC HỌC)

Module này tập trung vào **“Taking Stock of Stakeholders”** – tức là giai đoạn:

> 👉 Identify → Understand → Classify → Prioritize stakeholders

Đây là nền móng của **People Domain (~42% PMP)** và quyết định toàn bộ chất lượng engagement sau này.

---

### 📘 1. CORE IDEA: STAKEHOLDER IS THE SYSTEM (Domain: PEOPLE)

- Stakeholder không chỉ là “người liên quan”
- Họ là **hệ sinh thái ảnh hưởng dự án**

PM phải:
- Identify tất cả stakeholder (visible + hidden)
- Understand interest + influence
- Map relationships
- Prioritize engagement strategy

👉 Sai từ bước này → toàn bộ project lệch hướng

---

### 📘 2. STAKEHOLDER IDENTIFICATION (Domain: INITIATING)

Stakeholder có thể là:

- Internal: team, sponsor, PMO
- External: customer, supplier, regulator, community
- Hidden stakeholders: HOA, legal body, end users không rõ mặt

🚨 PMI trap:
❌ Chỉ nghĩ stakeholder = customer

👉 PMI correct mindset:
> “If they are affected → they are stakeholder”

---

### 📘 3. STAKEHOLDER ANALYSIS (Domain: PEOPLE)

Mục tiêu:
- Hiểu **ai quan trọng hơn ai**
- Hiểu **ai có thể gây rủi ro**
- Hiểu **ai cần engage trước**

---

## 📊 4. FIVE I’s FRAMEWORK (CORE EXAM ENGINE)

| I | Ý nghĩa | PMI Insight |
|---|--------|------------|
| Interest | Họ muốn gì | Expectation |
| Involvement | Mức tham gia | Engagement level |
| Impact | Ảnh hưởng lên họ | Benefit/Risk |
| Influence | Quyền lực | Control ability |
| Interdependencies | Quan hệ phụ thuộc | System complexity |

👉 Use:
- Prioritize stakeholders
- Design engagement strategy

---

### 📘 5. STAKEHOLDER MAPPING SYSTEM (Domain: PLANNING)

#### 📌 Power / Interest Grid
- High Power + High Interest → Manage closely
- High Power + Low Interest → Keep satisfied
- Low Power + High Interest → Keep informed
- Low Power + Low Interest → Monitor

👉 PMI trap:
❌ Treat all stakeholders equally → sai hoàn toàn

---

#### 📌 Directions of Influence
- Upward → sponsor, exec
- Downward → team, contractors
- Sideward → peers PM
- Outward → government, community, suppliers

👉 Insight:
> Influence is not linear → it is network-based

---

#### 📌 Stakeholder Cube (3D Model)
- Power
- Impact
- Attitude

👉 Value:
- Detect resistance early
- Identify hidden opposition

---

#### 📌 Salience Model
- Power
- Legitimacy
- Urgency

👉 Core rule:
> Highest overlap = highest priority stakeholder

---

### 📘 6. KEY ARTIFACT: STAKEHOLDER REGISTER (Domain: PROCESS)

Includes:
- Name
- Role
- Influence
- Interest
- Attitude
- Engagement level

👉 Value:
- Single source of truth for stakeholder system
- Basis for engagement plan

---

### 📘 7. OUTPUT: STAKEHOLDER ENGAGEMENT PLAN (FOUNDATION)

Defines:
- How to engage each stakeholder group
- Communication strategy
- Frequency of interaction
- Conflict handling approach

---

### 📘 8. PMI CORE PRINCIPLE

> “You cannot manage stakeholders you have not identified properly”

# 📘 3.3 HIGH-PERFORMING TEAMS LEAD TO HIGH PERFORMING PROJECTS

## 🔑 3.3 CORE INSIGHT
- High-performing teams = nền tảng của project success
- Team motivation ảnh hưởng trực tiếp đến:
  - Productivity
  - Engagement
  - Quality delivery
  - Commitment

---

## 🧠 MOTIVATION SYSTEM (PM VIEW)

### 🔹 Intrinsic Motivation
- Internal drive
- Meaning, growth, mastery
- Ownership & creativity
- Strong trong Agile environment

### 🔹 Extrinsic Motivation
- Rewards, bonus, recognition
- External incentives
- Short-term performance boost

---

## 🧠 RECOGNITION VS REWARDS

### 🎯 Recognition (Phi tài chính)
- Praise, feedback, appreciation
- Public hoặc private
- Tăng intrinsic motivation

### 💰 Rewards (Tài chính / vật chất)
- Bonus, promotion, time-off
- Gắn với KPI / milestone
- Tăng extrinsic motivation

---

## 📘 KEY MOTIVATION THEORIES (EXAM CORE)

- Maslow → nhu cầu theo tầng (physiological → self-actualization)
- McClelland → Achievement / Affiliation / Power
- Herzberg → Hygiene vs Motivators
- McGregor:
  - Theory X → control & supervision
  - Theory Y → trust & empowerment
  - Theory Z → long-term collaboration culture

---

## 📌 KEY INSIGHT
👉 PM không “ép team làm việc”  
👉 PM tạo môi trường để team **muốn làm việc**

# 📘 3.4 AGILE RESOURCING & TEAM DEVELOPMENT

## 🔑 AGILE TEAM CORE CHARACTERISTICS

- Self-organizing
- Cross-functional
- Small size (3–9 members)
- Collaborative
- Customer-focused
- Continuous improvement

---

## 👥 TEAM STRUCTURE

### 🔹 I-Shaped
- Deep expertise 1 domain
- Specialist role

### 🔹 T-Shaped
- Deep + broad skills
- Collaboration across domains

👉 Agile prefers: **T-shaped / Generalizing specialists**

## 🧠 DECISION MAKING TECHNIQUES

- Roman Voting (thumbs up/down/side)

- Fist of Five (0–5 agreement scale)

## 🧠 SERVANT LEADERSHIP (AGILE CORE)

- PM = facilitator, not commander
- Focus:
  - Remove blockers
  - Support team growth
  - Enable autonomy
- Outcome:
  - Trust ↑
  - Ownership ↑
  - Performance ↑

---

## 📈 SHU HA RI MODEL

- **Shu** → Follow rules (foundation)
- **Ha** → Adapt & experiment
- **Ri** → Mastery & innovation

👉 Agile maturity is **cyclical, not linear**

---

## 🧠 AGILE MINDSET

- Embrace change
- Customer-focused delivery
- Continuous improvement
- Collaboration over hierarchy
- Feedback-driven development

# 📘 3.5 HOW DO WE KNOW OUR TEAMS ARE DOING WELL?

## 🔑 CORE IDEA
👉 Evaluate team performance = check **outcomes, not just output**

---

## 📊 3 KEY PERFORMANCE CHECKS

### 1️⃣ Shared Ownership
- Team feels responsible for success
- Supported by:
  - Team Charter
  - Clear roles
  - Collective accountability

---

### 2️⃣ High-Performing Team Indicators
- Strong communication
- Clear ground rules
- Collaboration under pressure
- Fast issue resolution
- Adaptability to change

---

### 3️⃣ Leadership & Interpersonal Skills
- Servant leadership observed in team behavior
- Team members:
  - Take initiative
  - Support others
  - Solve problems collaboratively

---

## 🧰 KEY ARTIFACTS

- Team Charter → goals + roles + alignment
- Ground Rules → behavior + communication norms
- Agile ceremonies:
  - Daily Standup
  - Sprint Review
  - Retrospective

---

## 📌 PERFORMANCE SIGNALS (EXAM TRAP)

✔ Good sign:

- Self-organizing behavior
- Open communication

❌ Bad sign:
- Constant escalation to PM
- Confused responsibilities
- Low engagement

- Cross-functional collaboration occurs

- Solutions are co-created

# ⚡ MODULE: COMMUNICATIONS MANAGEMENT MASTERY (EXAM CORE SYSTEM)

---

## 🧠 SUMMARY (KIẾN THỨC + DOMAIN PMI ĐƯỢC HỌC)

Module này tập trung vào **Communications Management + Information + Knowledge Flow**, giúp PM hiểu cách:

> **Information → Knowledge → Communication → Value Delivery**

trong môi trường dự án hiện đại (Predictive + Agile).

---

### 📘 1. COMMUNICATIONS FOUNDATIONS (Domain: PEOPLE + PROCESS)

- Communication = trao đổi thông tin giữa PM, team, stakeholders  
- Mục tiêu: **shared understanding + alignment + decision-making**
- Stakeholder = bất kỳ ai bị ảnh hưởng bởi dự án
- Communication failure = risk trực tiếp tới project success

---

### 📘 2. COMMUNICATION REQUIREMENTS (Domain: PROCESS)

- Bước đầu tiên luôn là:
  > **Identify communication requirements**

- Trả lời 3 câu hỏi:
  - Ai cần thông tin?
  - Cần thông tin gì?
  - Khi nào & bằng cách nào?

💥 EXAM TRAP:
- ❌ Không chọn tools / engagement plan trước khi xác định nhu cầu

---

### 📘 3. COMMUNICATION METHODS (Domain: PROCESS + TOOLS)

- Push → gửi 1 chiều (email, report)
- Pull → tự truy cập (wiki, repository)
- Interactive → 2 chiều real-time (meeting, video call)

📌 Agile ưu tiên:
> Interactive communication

---

### 📘 4. COMMUNICATION MODEL (Domain: PEOPLE)

- Basic → sender → receiver
- Interactive → có feedback loop
- Complex → có **noise + emotion + bias**

📌 Noise gồm:
- distraction
- culture
- misunderstanding
- lack of knowledge

---

### 📘 5. COMMUNICATION TYPES (Domain: PEOPLE + PROCESS)

- Formal verbal → meetings, presentations  
- Informal verbal → chat, hallway talk  
- Formal written → reports, contracts  
- Informal written → email, message  

---

### 📘 6. INFORMATION vs KNOWLEDGE SYSTEM (Domain: BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT)

- Information = dữ liệu đã xử lý  
- Knowledge = hiểu + áp dụng + kinh nghiệm  

📌 Vòng lặp:

> Information → Communication → Knowledge → Documentation → Information

---

### 📘 7. KNOWLEDGE MANAGEMENT (Domain: BUSINESS ENVIRONMENT)

- Explicit knowledge → tài liệu hóa (plans, templates)
- Tacit knowledge → kinh nghiệm cá nhân

📌 Sharing methods:
- mentoring
- coaching
- lessons learned
- job rotation
- storytelling
- communities of practice

---

### 📘 8. AGILE COMMUNICATION PRACTICES (Domain: AGILE SYSTEM)

- Sprint Planning → define work
- Daily Standup → sync progress
- Sprint Review → demo + feedback
- Sprint Retrospective → improve process

📌 Agile = continuous communication loop

---

### 📘 9. COMMUNICATION FAILURE SIGNALS (Domain: CONTROL SYSTEM)

- Repeatedly asking same info
- One-off unexpected requests
- No response to requests

👉 Root cause:
> Communication plan không phù hợp stakeholder needs

---

### 📘 10. STAKEHOLDER ENGAGEMENT VS COMMUNICATIONS MANAGEMENT

| Engagement | Communication |
|------------|--------------|
| Relationship building | Information delivery |
| Manage expectations | Transmit data |
| Participation | Distribution |

💥 TRAP:
- Engagement ≠ sending reports
- Communication ≠ stakeholder management

---

### 📘 11. COMMUNICATION TECHNOLOGY (Domain: TOOLS)

- Video conferencing → real-time interaction  
- Slack / Teams → collaboration  
- SharePoint / Drive → document storage  
- Jira / Asana → tracking systems  

📌 Selection criteria:
- Speed
- Security
- Time zone
- Complexity
